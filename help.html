Welcome to the ThoughtWorks Hyderabad Tambola What is a Housie/Bingo/Tambola? Housie How to play? As a player, You can take upto 3 tickets. Just signup here and pay each ticket 20 rupees to admin. How to mark a number in the ticket? Get your tickets using /tickets command and click on the number you want to mark. How to win? We have transposed the Housie card due to few technical reasons. So, you will have to finish columns (rows in normal housie) and full housie. There are 4 categories First column, Second Coulmn, Third Column and Full housie. Each ticket contains claim buttons. Just click on it to claim the winning. Note: You will be kicked out of the game when you try to mark invalid numbers multiple times. /example - Example of the transposed ticket Commands: /help - To see this help /signup (1 or 2 or 3) - To signup to the game. Choose the number of tickets you want to play. /tickets - To see your tickets and play. /winners - To see the winners
